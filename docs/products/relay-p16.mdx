<TabItem value="overview">

When it comes to power isolation, this module comes in perfectly handy. The relays are optically-isolated from the rest of the circuit. The 16x relays are capable of up to 10A @ 250V each. The relays are powered from a 12V barrel-jack power source.

    if v > 0
