This is the ideal combo for [Stick](../microcomputer/stick) Microcomputer. It exposes all pins, some on male and some on female headers. The male headers add power and ground in a standard servo pinout". Signals and busses are clearly marked on the circuit. It also includes buttons and a regulator to complete the list of requirements to start making [your own modules](../third-party)!
