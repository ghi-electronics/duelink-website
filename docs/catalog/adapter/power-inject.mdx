When necessary, this module injects power in a long chain of DUELink modules. This also helps power any modules that have extra power needs than available on the main power source.

![Daisylink](pathname:///img/daisylink.png)

Power comes in over USB connection or a screw terminal block. On the USB side, this is just a USB connection. Use a phone charger or power back for power. On the terminal block, any DC source 4V to 12V. The module includes a 800mA regulator. Note that if higher voltage is used, like over 9V, and higher current used, over 500mA, the regulator will get a bit **too hot for the touch**. Use a lower voltage source, under 6V,  when needing higher currents, over 500mA.
