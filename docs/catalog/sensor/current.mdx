<TabItem value="overview">

Connect the terminal block inline of AC or DC power to measure its current. The sensor has an internal resistance of 1.2 milli-ohm. It is capable of measuring up to 12.5A. A higher current model is available that is capable of up to 25A.

:::warning
DO NOT mess with live AC power! You will get hurt or worse if you do not know what you are doing!
:::
