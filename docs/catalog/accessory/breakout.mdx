This module can be used as a DUELink cable extender. It also exposes all DUELink cable signals on a female header. This is useful to wire a [Supported Hardware](../../hw/intro) that does not have a JST connector.

The DRP and CTN signals are a mix of [UART](../../interface/i2c), [I2C](../../interface/uart) and [USB](../../interface/usb) [Interfaces](../../interface/intro).
