<TabItem value="overview">

When it comes to power isolation, this module comes in perfectly handy. The relays are optically-isolated from the rest of the circuit. The 16x relays are capable of up to 10A @ 250V each. The relays are powered from a 12V barrel-jack power source. Use a regulated 5V transformer, capable of 500mA or higher. Note the module will continue to function without this power source, but the relays will not work. An indicator LED shows the power source availability.

:::warning
Do NOT mess with live AC power! You will get hurt or worse if you do not know what you are doing!
:::
