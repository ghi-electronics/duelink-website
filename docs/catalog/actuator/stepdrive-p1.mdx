<TabItem value="overview">
This module utilizes the very common stepper motor drivers commonly used in Arduino CNC Shield. They are available in multiple models. We include a driver capable of 30V @ 1A, but you can easily swap it with your favorite driver if desired.

Two terminal blocks are installed for wiring the power source and the stepper motor, no soldering is necessary!
