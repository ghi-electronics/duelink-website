<TabItem value="overview">

Control high current loads using these 4x mechanical relays. Each relay is capable of driving up to 1A at 250V. The relays are powered from a 5V barrel-jack power source. Use a regulated 5V transformer, capable of 100mA or higher. Note the module will continue to function without this power source, but the relays will not work. An indicator LED shows the power source availability.

:::warning
Do NOT mess with live AC power! You will get hurt or worse if you do not know what you are doing!
:::
