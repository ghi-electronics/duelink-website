##### Fingerprint readme #####

1. Make sure the status LED is blinking.
2. Wait until the red light on the fingerprint sensor is blinking, indicating it is ready.
3. Place your finger on the surface.
4. The light will turn purple, indicating the fingerprint sensor is scanning your finger.
5. Wait until the light turns blue, indicating a valid fingerprint has been detected.
6. While the light is purple, you may need to gently move your finger for optimal detection.
7. The test is considered passed only when the light turns blue.
