##### USB HOOK #####

1. Make sure PC has speaker connected, and volume is on
2. Conneck USB Hook D socket to the wire has NO knot from the test kit.
3. Connect PC to USB Hook by USB cable directly. 
4. After connected about 1 second, the Programmer will show two pictures: USB hook on left side, OLED 096 on right side.
5. Disconect usb cable, the picture on right side will show "No device connected"
6. PC will play connect/disconnect sound every time connect/disconnect USB Hook.
